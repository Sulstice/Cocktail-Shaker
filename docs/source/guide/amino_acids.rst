Amino Acids

Below is a list of the amino acids that cocktail-shaker currently supports.

+------------------+---------------------------+
| Amino Acid       | SMILES                    |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Alanine          | C                         |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Arginine         | CCCCNC(N)=N               |

| Asparagine       | CCC(N)=O                  |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Aspartic Acid    | CC(O)=O                   |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Cysteine         | CS                        |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Glutamic Acid    | CCC(O)=O                  |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Glutamine        | CCC(N)=O                  |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Glycine          | [H]                       |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Histidine        | CC1=CNC=N1                |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Isoleucine       | C(CC)([H])C               |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Leucine          | CC(C)C                    |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Lysine           | CCCCN                     |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Methionine       | CCSC                      |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| PhenylAlanine    | CC1=CC=CC=C1              |
+------------------+---------------------------+
| Proline          | TBD                       |

| Serine           | CO                        |

| Threonine        | C(C)([H])O                |
